Privacy Policy - BRCS

Effective date: 2026-04-09

App name: BRCS

Developer: Marlou

This Privacy Policy explains how BRCS handles information when you use the app.

Summary

  • No account required: BRCS does not require online account registration.
  • Offline-first: BRCS is designed to work offline and stores data locally on your device.
  • No selling of data: We do not sell your personal information.

Information the app may access

Depending on the features you use, BRCS may request access to:

  • Camera (QR scanning): Used to scan QR codes inside the app.
  • Photos / Storage (optional): Used only if you choose to upload or attach an image.

Data collected and how it is used

BRCS primarily stores information locally on your device to provide core features.

Local app data (stored on device)

  • What: Admin-created records such as customer/driver profiles, points, transactions, promotions, and related metadata.
  • Where: Stored locally in the app database on your device (SQLite).
  • Why: To operate the rewards system and show dashboards, reports, and exports.

QR scanning data

  • What: QR content you scan.
  • Why: To identify or validate records for rewards/redemptions.
  • Where: Used within the app and stored locally only as needed.

Images (if you use photo features)

  • What: Photos you select or capture.
  • Why: To attach them to records managed in BRCS.
  • Where: Stored locally on your device.

Sharing / exporting

If you export or share a file (PDF/CSV), the app hands that file to the share target you choose (for example email or messaging app). That third-party app/service may process your data under its own policies.

Data sharing

BRCS does not share your data with third parties by default. Sharing occurs only when you explicitly choose to export or share a file.

Data retention and deletion

  • Data is kept on your device until you delete it in the app or uninstall the app.
  • Uninstalling the app generally removes app-local data from that device.

Children's privacy

BRCS is intended for business/admin use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards within the app's design (local storage and OS protections). No storage method is 100% secure. Protect your device with a screen lock and avoid sharing exports containing sensitive information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the app. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
